About the Project

Find My Supervisor exists to address the difficulty in finding supervisors or mentors for research projects at the University of Glasgow. While best suited for pairing people intending to carry out self defined projects, the system allows for projects to be added by an administrators - should supervisors be inclined to do so.

The web application was conceived and developed by Lama Assum, Kris Camilleri, Yi Zhou, and Carol Stewart. This was carried out as part of the Internet Technologies module, lectured by Leif Azzopardi at the University of Glasgow in 2015.

The project is open source and further development is welcomed at https://github.com/lamaassum/ITECH/.
